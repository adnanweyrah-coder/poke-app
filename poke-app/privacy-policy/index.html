Privacy Policy

POKE: Space Survivor · Last updated: May 2026

POKE: Space Survivor ("the App") is developed by Adnan Said ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

POKE: Space Survivor is designed with privacy in mind. The App operates primarily on your device and collects minimal data:

  • Local game data: Your scores, badges, XP, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No account required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to play.
  • No location data: We do not collect your location.
  • No camera or microphone: We do not access your camera or microphone.

2. Analytics

We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics (such as crash reports provided by the platform — Apple App Store or Google Play) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Advertising

The current version of POKE: Space Survivor does not display third-party advertisements. If we introduce optional rewarded advertisements in a future version, we will update this policy and notify users accordingly.

4. In-App Purchases

Any in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

POKE: Space Survivor is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since no personal information is collected at all, the App is safe for children to use.

6. Data Storage and Security

All game progress is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Fonts (for typography) which may log standard server access data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
